Data month-by-monthÂ¶

The observations are distributed in monthly files. Each line in a file is a single observation, and the columns are:

  1. Year
  2. Month (1-12)
  3. Day of month (1-31)
  4. Hour of day (0-23)
  5. Minute of hour (0-59)
  6. Latitude (degrees north)
  7. Longitude (degrees East of Greenwich)
  8. Observation (hPa for mslp)
  9. Station name
